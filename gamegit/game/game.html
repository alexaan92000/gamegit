The Issue

In a study done in 1995, 1,000 women were interviewed on the topic of emotional abuse. 36% of the women were emotionally abused while growing up. 39% of the women were emotionally abused in a relationship for the past five years. In a study done 5 years later 35% of all women who are or have been married have experienced emotional abuse. Emotional abuse is responsible for long term problems with health, self esteem, depression and anxiety.

Our Mission

Our mission is to spread awareness of emotional and verbal abuse so that people are able to recognize the signs and be able to leave those harmful relationships.

Logistics of the game

In our online game you are talking to a friend, you get to chose between 2 dialogue options. Fighting will make the person turn against you, you must go through the conversation entirely and leave calmly to "win" the game.
